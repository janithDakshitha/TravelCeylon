The Pearl Of The Indian Ocean

Sri Lanka, historically known as Ceylon, is a South Asian island country its official name is the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka.Sri Lanka is one of the world's 34 biodiversity hotspots, with the most biodiversity per unit area of land among Asian nations. Almost all of the country's woody endemic plants and roughly 75% of its endemic animals live in the wet zone rainforests.

Tourism in Sri Lanka

Tourism is a major industry in Sri Lanka. The stunning beaches, lush forests, and incredible archaeological sites that can be found in Sri Lanka make it a dream destination for travelers from all over the world. Both the warmheartedness of the local population and the pleasant year-round weather attract a lot of visitors to Sri Lanka.
